Research Notes
Objective

The objective of this research was to identify manufacturing companies that closely match DeepThought's Ideal Customer Profile (Federer ICP).

Research Strategy

The research was performed using a structured approach rather than random company searches.

Step 1 – Industry Selection

The following industries were selected because of their strong manufacturing ecosystem in Hyderabad:

Specialty Biotech
Specialty Chemicals
Medical Devices
Precision Engineering
Industrial Electronics
Step 2 – Company Discovery

Potential companies were identified through:

Official company websites
LinkedIn
MCA records
Industry reports
Google Search
Company directories
Annual reports
Investor presentations
Step 3 – Information Collection

For every company, the following information was collected:

Products manufactured
Manufacturing facilities
Revenue band
Decision makers
Industry segment
Growth indicators
Certifications
Leadership structure
Step 4 – Evaluation

Each company was evaluated using the Federer framework based on:

Producer status
Accessibility
Product differentiation
Leadership quality
Industry growth
Company growth signals
Systems maturity
Leadership succession
Step 5 – Documentation

The final shortlisted companies were documented in the spreadsheet along with:

Federer score
Verdict
Personalization hook
Supporting sources
Challenges Faced
Limited availability of revenue information for private companies.
Different companies disclose different levels of public information.
Some companies required verification from multiple sources before evaluation.
Outcome

A structured shortlist of companies was prepared using publicly available information and evaluated consistently using the Federer scoring framework.
